Soviet songs

This page used to be for the Soviet Anthem only, but I added 2 other songs, all from the Soviet Union. These songs are the nicest ones that I can find, having an excellent melody and a positive message.

Due to GitHub restrictions on website traffic, I am unable to put the audio files here without compromising the performance of my website. Therefore for each song I will leave a link to YouTube for you to listen to the song. If you want the audio file, use a YouTube downloader. If you cannot access YouTube due to your country's internet restrictions, message me for the audio files. I also have 30min long study music versions of these songs that you can message me to get.
