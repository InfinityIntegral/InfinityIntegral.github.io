Contest timer

This is a timer to be used for school mock examinations and Olympiad mock contests. After entering the contest name and duration (in minutes), a timer will start. The colour of the text showing the amount of time remaining will gradually change from pink to blue as the time runs out. After the time ends, a notification will appear and music will start playing.
