Catch the circle

This is a video game that I have coded. You can play the game at this link, but not make any copies of the game. You are STRICTLY FORBIDDEN from playing this game during school hours unless given permission by a teacher.

The objective of the game is to press the randomly moving blue circle to gain points. The circle's size gradually decrease, reaching 0 after 100 successful hits. When you have completed the game by getting 100 points, your time taken will be displayed, which you can take a screenshot of to show your friends.
