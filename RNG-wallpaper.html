random wallpaper generator

Using this app, you can create random wallpapers for your devices. After inputting a wallpaper style and your device aspect ratio, a image will appear. Take a screenshot of the image and you will be able to set it as your new wallpaper.

What style of wallpaper do you want? If you do not know which, generate one for both and pick your preferred version.

What is the screen aspect ratio of your device? For most phones, it is 9:16, and for most computers or tablets, it is 3:4. Only these 2 aspect ratios are supported by this app for now.
